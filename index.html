About Alice

Alice is a yoga teacher inspired by her Chinese heritage and a philosophy that values balance in body, mind, and emotions. She discovered yoga at university during busy exam seasons in 2011 and has since developed a practice focused on self-awareness, personal growth, and meaningful connection.

Having lived in different countries, Alice brings a deep appreciation for diverse cultures and loves weaving curiosity and conversation into her classes. She creates a space where students can fully focus on the mat, leaving behind what holds them back. Through alignment-focused, mindful sequences, she guides students of all levels to build strength, manage stress, and cultivate harmony in both their practice and daily life.

Classes

MoreYoga Studio Classes
